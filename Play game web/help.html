Help & Support

1. How can I participate in a game?

Go to the “Participation Form” section, choose your game, and fill in your details to apply.

2. How can I check my score?

Open the “My Score” page from the top menu to view your latest results and achievements.

3. How do I contact the YourPlay team?

You can use the Enquiry page to send questions or feedback directly to our support team.

4. I forgot my username or password. What should I do?

Visit the Settings page and select “Change Username” or “Change Password” to update your details.
